Our Story

KatoKits was born from the frustration of spending countless hours creating lesson plans and activities for preschoolers. As educators and parents ourselves, we knew there had to be a better way.

We combined our deep understanding of early childhood development with cutting-edge AI technology to create a platform that generates high-quality, personalized lesson plans in seconds rather than hours.

Today, KatoKits serves thousands of educators, homeschooling families, and childcare centers worldwide, saving them time while improving learning outcomes for children ages 6 months to 6 years.
